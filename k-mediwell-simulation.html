Chapter 1 · Gi (氣) and the Brain — Where East Meets West

For millennia, Eastern medicine has regarded gi (氣) — the flow of vital energy within the human body — as the foundation of all healing. In 2022, Harvard Medical School's Dr. Christopher Palmer proposed in Brain Energy that all mental illness stems from metabolic disorders of the brain. The convergence of these two frameworks is striking.

"When the energy production system collapses, humans fall mentally ill." — Christopher M. Palmer, MD (Harvard)

In a 2008 rat sepsis trial, subjects receiving energy therapy showed 100% survival. The control group experienced total mortality. This data forms the scientific backbone of the K-MediWell ebook platform.
